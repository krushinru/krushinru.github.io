## About

HabitHit is a Telegram Mini App for tracking habits, launched in 2025. It helps people build and maintain useful routines through a simple interface inside the messenger.

HabitHit tackles a common motivation problem: people often give up when they cannot see progress. It makes streaks and results visible, which makes it easier to keep going.

### Key features

**Visual tracker**

HabitHit shows progress in weekly, monthly, and yearly views. This helps users see their momentum and understand how consistently they are working toward their goals.

**Data privacy**

Habit data is stored in each user's personal Telegram CloudStorage. That keeps the data private and avoids sending it to third-party servers.

**Cross-platform access**

Because the app runs inside Telegram, habits stay in sync across a user's devices without extra setup. Mark a habit as done on a phone, and the update is immediately visible on desktop.

### Results

HabitHit is actively developed and already used by Telegram users to track daily habits. It works as a full web application inside the messenger and does not require installing a separate app.
