## About

HabitHit is a Telegram Mini App for tracking habits, launched in 2025. It helps people build and maintain useful routines through a simple interface inside the messenger.

The project solves a motivation problem: many people quit because they cannot see progress. HabitHit gives them a clear visual picture of their streaks and results, making it easier to keep going.

### Key features

**Visual tracker**  
The service shows habit progress with weekly, monthly, and yearly views. This helps users see their momentum and understand how consistently they are working toward their goals.

**Private data**  
Habit data is stored in the user's personal Telegram CloudStorage. This keeps the data private and avoids sending it to third-party servers.

**Cross-platform access**  
Because the app works inside Telegram, habits are available on all of a user's devices without extra synchronization. Mark something done on the phone, and the progress is visible on desktop.

### Results

The project is in active development and is used by Telegram users to track daily habits. It works as a full web application inside the messenger and does not require installing another app.
