## About

ClubStat launched at the height of Clubhouse's popularity in early 2021, when the audio social network was gaining millions of users during the pandemic. It automatically collected up-to-date data on the platform's most popular rooms, users, and clubs.

### Key features

**User rankings**

The system tracked the most influential Russian-speaking Clubhouse users. Rankings updated in real time based on activity and audience engagement.

**Room and club analytics**

ClubStat indexed tens of thousands of rooms created on the platform every day, tracking popular topics, room schedules, and participant counts. It helped people discover worthwhile conversations about business, startups, finance, marketing, and technology.

### Results

During its run, ClubStat became one of the main discovery tools for the Russian-speaking Clubhouse community. It helped thousands of users find interesting conversations, follow popular speakers, and gave advertisers a practical way to choose placements.

Active development stopped after Clubhouse's popularity dropped sharply in June 2021. The platform could not retain its pandemic-era audience once Twitter Spaces and similar features appeared across other social networks.
